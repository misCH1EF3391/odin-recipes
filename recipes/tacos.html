Chicken Tacos

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of chicken breasts cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1/2 teaspoon of chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of grocund cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • l/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 8-10 corn tortillas
  • shredded cheese
  • chopped cilantro, tomatoes, avocado, sour cream

    • Instructions

    Step 1
    Prepare the chicken coating it well with the chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper.
    Step 2
    Cook the chicken on a cast iron skillet in oil.
    Step 3
    Shred the chicken to bite sized pieces.
    Step 4
    Wrap the tortillas in a damp paper towel and microwave for 20-30 seconds. Or grill them.
    Step 5
    Assemble the tacos and enjoy!
