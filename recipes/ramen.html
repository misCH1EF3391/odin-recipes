Ramen Time

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of ramen noodles with flavor packet
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon of butter
  • 1/4 teaspoon of toasted sesame seeds
  • thinly sliced scallions

Step 1
Cook the ramen noodles in boiling water according to package instructions.
Step 2
Add flavor packet and stir.
Step 3
Crack an egg into the hot noodles and let it cook for 1 minute.
Step 4
Add butter and sesame seeds. Mix well.
Step 5
Garnish with scallions.
